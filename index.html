Welcome to KMNx Clan

Standoff 2 isn’t just another mobile FPS – it’s a game that built its own community over the years. Since its release, Standoff 2 has given players fast-paced, tactical gameplay with maps and modes that feel just as intense as on PC shooters. From casual matches to competitive tournaments, it’s a game that rewards skill, teamwork, and strategy.

Our clan, KMNx, was formed out of that same passion for the game. We started as a small group of friends who just loved playing together, grinding ranks late into the night. Over time, more players joined, and what was once a small squad turned into a growing community of competitive and casual players. Today, KMNx stands for more than just gaming – it’s about teamwork, respect, and improving together.

Whether you’re here to compete at the highest level or just to enjoy Standoff 2 with friends, KMNx is a place where you’ll feel at home.
