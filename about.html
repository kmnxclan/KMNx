About KMNx Clan

KMNx Clan is a professional gaming clan built for players who want to dominate the battlefield and have fun while doing it. Founded by SNIPEx and co-led by MATRIx, our team thrives on strategy, skill, and teamwork.

Our goal is to create a community where players can grow, compete, and enjoy high-level gameplay. We support each other both in-game and off-game and welcome anyone passionate about gaming.

From casual matches to tournaments, KMNx Clan provides a space where every player can improve, contribute, and become part of a professional environment. Join us, be part of the legacy, and level up with the best!
